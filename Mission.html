Vision & Mission

VISION:

To be a world class police service, with a people-friendly, responsive and professional workforce.

MISSION:

We are committed to providing T quality police service to meet the expectations of our customers; by upholding the rule of law, creating and maintaining strong partnerships for a conducive social, economic and political development of Kenya.

CORE VALUES:

Be proactive and responsive in the discharge of our duties:
o To exercise integrity and courtesy at all time;
o To cultivate and maintain partnership with all stakeholders;
o To create and maintain team spirit within the service;
o To be fair and firm in all our undertakings;
o To maintain a disciplined and professional workforce;
o To be gender sensitive; and
o To promote, protect and respect the human rights of our customers.

CORE FUNCTIONS:

o Maintenance of law and order;
o Preservation of peace;
o Protection of life and property;
o Prevention and detection of crime;
o Apprehension of offenders; and
o Enforcement of all laws and regulations with which it has been charged.
